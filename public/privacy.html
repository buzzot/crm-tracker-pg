Privacy Policy

Samyou CRM — Last updated: July 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Samyou USA ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Samyou CRM ("the application").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, and any data you enter into the application such as contacts, companies, deals, projects, and activity records. We also collect authentication information via Airtable OAuth when you sign in.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to operate and provide the features of Samyou CRM. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

All CRM data is stored in Airtable on your organization's behalf. We do not maintain a separate copy of your data outside of Airtable's infrastructure. Airtable's own privacy practices apply to data stored within their platform.

4. Authentication

Samyou CRM uses Airtable OAuth for authentication. We receive your Airtable account email and name for the purpose of identifying you within the application. We do not store your Airtable password.

5. Cookies and Sessions

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. These cookies are necessary for the application to function and are not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure session handling. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Access and Control

You may request deletion of your data or revoke the application's access to your Airtable account at any time via your Airtable account settings under Integrations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at info@samyouusa.com.
